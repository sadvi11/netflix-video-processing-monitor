# WHY I Built This Project

## The Canadian Business Problem
Netflix Canada processes 10,000 video uploads daily.
Each video goes through transcoding, quality checks, and CDN distribution.
When something fails silently — customers get buffering errors at 8pm Friday.

This monitoring system catches failures BEFORE customers notice.

## What It Monitors
- Jobs started vs completed vs failed
- Transcoding duration per job
- Quality score per video
- Real-time CloudWatch dashboard
- Automatic alerts when failure rate exceeds threshold

## Nokia Connection
At Nokia I monitored 5G packet processing pipelines — millions of
packets per second. Same principle: measure everything, alert on
anomalies, fix before users are affected.
